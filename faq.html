Frequently Asked Questions

"Do you take commissions?"

The answer will be always YES, i usually have between 2-7 slots every month, with paid reservations available with one month of anticipation.

What languages do you speak?

Spanish and English.

What kind of work do you do?

I do everything from a charming chibi to what others call "disturbing", which i don't, i'll always find it interesting!
-18 / +18 / yuri GL / yaoi BL / vore / female transformation / feet / scat / guro and more. .

What kind of jobs do you NOT do?

Only chubby and related (personal trauma) (pregnancy and vore extensions don't bother me).

If I want a commission what should I do?

First you should check this page: COMMISSION DETAILS
Please check the important details section to avoid misunderstandings.

Then send me an email to: aya_yany@hotmail.com or a twitter message to @aya_yany with everything about the commission, from your questions to the details, i don't judge.

Do all payments have to be in full upfront at the beginning?

Yes, for single piece cases, for monthly pieces each piece can be paid at the beginning of the month where it will be worked on, or reserved the month prior.

What is your currency?

All payments are in USD dollars with 5.9% paypal fee.

How long should I wait to see the finished commission?

I will mention you the exact timings before the payment, usually it is one month, since I work on several comics and I only have weekends for freelance slots.
In the case of reservations it's 2 months, but at least the slot will be secured.

How can I contact you?

Via email: aya_yany@hotmail.com or twitter message to https://twitter.com/aya_yany.

Why do you use emojis or smilies?

Because when i have to explain details, or how I work I sound bitter... (- ▽ -;) it's hard to speak professionally without sounding rude.

Are there any returns?

No, I've never done any, that's why it's very important to check the following page: COMMISSION DETAILS

Can I make a long term manga?

Yes, it can be as long as you want, for that I have packages, each chapter can have a basic free cover.
